Why Summer of Bitcoin

My interest in Bitcoin is centered on understanding the system at a protocol and network level rather than price movements. I am familiar with the UTXO model, transaction structure, and how transactions are validated and ordered into blocks.

Through building Bitcoin-related tools and contributing to open source, I want to deepen my understanding of Bitcoin internals while contributing meaningful improvements to production Bitcoin software through Summer of Bitcoin.
